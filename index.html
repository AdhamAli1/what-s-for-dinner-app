Teriyaki Chicken Bowl

Sweet and savory chicken over rice with vegetables

  • 1 400g chicken thighs, sliced
  • 2 1/2 cup teriyaki sauce
  • 3 2 cups cooked rice
  • 4 1 broccoli head, florets
  • 5 1 carrot, julienned
  • 6 Sesame seeds
  • 7 Green onions, sliced
  • 8 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  1. 1 Heat sesame oil in a pan. Cook chicken until browned on all sides.
  2. 2 Add teriyaki sauce to chicken, simmer for 5 minutes until sauce thickens.
  3. 3 Meanwhile, steam broccoli and carrots until tender-crisp.
  4. 4 Divide rice between bowls.
  5. 5 Top with teriyaki chicken and steamed vegetables.
